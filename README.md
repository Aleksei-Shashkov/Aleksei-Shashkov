### 🚀 What drives me

* 🔍 **Uncovering the "Why"**: I love turning messy data into clear, actionable stories that drive business decisions.
* 🧠 **Analytical Curiosity**: I'm passionate about exploring patterns and finding the hidden logic behind the numbers.
* 🧩 **Visual Clarity**: I enjoy building intuitive reports and systems that make complex information easy to digest.
* 🤖 **Automation Mindset**: I'm interested in using AI Agents and automated workflows to streamline data analysis tasks.

### 🧪 My Approach to Analysis

1. **Break Down Complexity**: Deconstructing large business problems into small, manageable analytical questions.
2. **Data Transformation**: Cleaning and restructuring data to ensure a reliable "source of truth".
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Identifying trends, correlations, and outliers to guide the strategy.
4. **Insight Delivery**: Presenting results through Python-based visualizations and evidence-based recommendations.
